# HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition using Power BI.
The goal is to identify key factors influencing employee turnover and provide insights that can help improve retention.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze overall attrition rate in the company
* Identify departments and job roles with high attrition
* Understand the impact of salary, experience, and age on attrition
* Provide insights for better workforce management

---

## Dataset

The dataset used is an HR dataset containing employee information such as:

* Age
* Department
* Job Role
* Monthly Income
* Job Satisfaction
* Work Life Balance
* Years at Company
* Attrition (1 = Yes, 0 = No)

---

## Data Preprocessing

The following preprocessing steps were performed:

* Removed unnecessary columns (EmployeeCount, StandardHours, etc.)
* Created Age Groups
* Created Experience Groups (Entry, Junior, Mid, Senior)
* Created Income Groups
* Handled data types and ensured clean data

---

## KPIs

The dashboard includes the following key performance indicators:

* Total Employees
* Attrition Count
* Attrition Rate (%)
* Average Salary

---

## Visualizations

The following charts are included in the dashboard:

* Attrition by Salary
* Attrition by Job Role
* Attrition by Department
* Attrition by Age Group
* Attrition by Experience (Years at Company)
* Attrition by Levels

---

## Key Insights

* Overall attrition rate is around 16%
* Higher attrition observed in entry-level employees
* Employees with lower salary show higher attrition
* Sales and R&D departments have higher attrition
* Job roles like Sales Executive show higher turnover

---

## Tools Used

* Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboard)

---

## Conclusion

This dashboard provides a clear overview of employee attrition patterns and helps identify areas that require attention.
It can support decision-making related to employee retention, compensation, and workforce planning.
